JPEG
Large files that do not require transparent backgrounds or animations. This format is ideal for vivid photorealistic photographs as it can contain million of colors.
PNG
Has an additional parameter for discribing transparency - the alpha channel. Suitable for image width transparent ot monochrome backgrounds. Used for icons and decorative items.
webP
A replacement format for PNG and JPEG, but with incomplete browser support. When equal image quality, the compressed files will besmaller than PNG and JPEG on average by 25%.
